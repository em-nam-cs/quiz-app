Welcome to the quiz flashcard game. 

Press START to start the game. 

A question will be written at the top of the card and each possible answer
will be written in a blue answer button. Click on the correct answer and get a 
point! After you choose, the correct answer(s) will be shown as green and the 
wrong answers will be shown as red. Press NEXT or the Enter Key to advance 
to the next question.

After all the questions are answered, you can click RESTART to play again!
